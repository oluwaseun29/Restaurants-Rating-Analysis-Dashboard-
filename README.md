# Restaurants-Rating-Analysis-Dashboard-

Introduction

This project analyzes restaurant ratings, customer demographics, cuisine preferences, and investment opportunities using Power BI. The goal is to identify the factors that influence customer satisfaction, understand consumer behavior, and highlight areas where investors and restaurant owners can make better business decisions.
The dashboard combines restaurant ratings, customer demographics, preferred cuisines, and location data to provide meaningful insights that support strategic planning and investment.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze overall restaurant performance based on customer ratings.
Evaluate food and service quality.
Identify the most preferred cuisines.
Understand customer demographics and spending patterns.
Discover demand and supply gaps across cuisines.
Provide recommendations for restaurant owners and investors.

🔍 Key Insights

Restaurant Performance

The average overall restaurant rating is 1.20 out of 2, indicating moderate customer satisfaction.
Food quality received the lowest average rating (1.
00), suggesting there is room for improvement.
Service quality (1.09) is close to the overall rating, showing that customer service plays an important role in satisfaction.
Some restaurants consistently perform better than others, indicating noticeable differences in quality.

Customer Demographics

About 68% of customers belong to the medium-budget category.
Most customers are single.
The largest customer group falls within the 20–35 years age range.
San Luis Potosi has the highest concentration of customers.

Demand and Supply Gap

Mexican cuisine has one of the highest numbers of restaurants.
Some cuisines have strong customer demand but relatively few restaurants.
These underserved cuisines represent attractive investment opportunities.

Investment Insights

Cities such as Cuernavaca and Jiutepec show relatively high average restaurant ratings.
Restaurant success appears to be strongly influenced by food quality, service quality, and customer cuisine preferences.

💡 Recommendations

For Restaurant Owners
Improve food quality to increase customer satisfaction.
Maintain excellent customer service standards.
Align menu offerings with customer preferences.
Regularly monitor customer feedback to improve performance.
For Investors
Invest in cuisines with high customer demand but limited competition.
Prioritize cities with stronger average restaurant ratings.
Target the young adult and medium-budget customer segment for better business opportunities.

📈 Conclusion

This analysis shows that the restaurant market is largely driven by young, single, and medium-budget consumers. Customer satisfaction depends heavily on both food quality and service quality. Investors can maximize growth by focusing on high-demand cuisines with limited competition and expanding into cities with stronger restaurant ratings. These insights can help restaurant owners improve customer experience while guiding investors toward profitable opportunities.
